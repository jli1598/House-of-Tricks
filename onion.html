Y U M ,   C A N D Y  A P P L E S





Mmm-mmmm. Sweet, delicious candied apples. Here, try one.

This is a very funny prank for April fools! If you really have someone to prank and don't know what to do, than I can assure you that this is a very good prank because it is super easy!


Materials needed: A Bowl, An Onion, Caramel Squares (or any candy you prefer), Wooden Sticks


STEP ONE: Melt the Caramel

Put all the caramel squares in a bowl, and put it in the microwave for about 30 seconds, or until it turns liquidy.


STEP TWO: Place the Sticks

Now take your apple and onion and poke a stick at the bottom of the apple and onion. Be careful while poking a stick threw the onion! Be gentle because the onion might break easily.



              BEGINNER

STEP THREE: Caramelize the Apples

Now take your apple and onion and cover it with caramel. Role it around in circles so that the apple and onion are fully covered with caramel. Put them into the freezer until they're frozen (but still edible).
